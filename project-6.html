Dungeon Bites

Game UI Project

What if cooking was your greatest weapon? What if the ingredients you needed to save your restaurant were found deep within dangerous dungeons, full of monsters and adventure?

Setting

The game takes place in a kingdom where dungeons are living, growing ecosystems. Adventurers regularly raid dungeons to gather treasures, rare materials, and magical artifacts to sell, but monsters are seen as dangerous pests rather than potential food. The idea of eating dungeon creatures is considered absurd (and mildly horrifying) by most people.
