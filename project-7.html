Wander Scouts

Branded Web Expereince Project

Wander Scouts is app for children ages 5 and up to encourage exploration of local landscapes and teach children how to navigate the local wild, as well as how to properly handle animal encounters. We believe that instilling this knowledge from a young age creates more well informed adults, which in turn contributes to the conservation and protection of natural habitats. We also want to nurture kid’s curiosity and create opportunities for parents and kids to bond while learning alongside each other.

The problem statement we aim to address is “How can we teach families to be more aware of their surrounds and be safe around local suburban wildlife, coexist, and preserve their habitat?”
