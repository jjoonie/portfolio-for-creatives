Second Slice

Second Slice’s program aims to empower at-risk youth by teaching them valuable baking skills, providing them with an outlet for creativity, and offering opportunities for personal and professional growth. Participants will learn the fundamentals of baking, including pastry and cake making, while also gaining practical knowledge about entrepreneurship, including how to sell their creations for profit. Through this program, they will develop a sense of accomplishment, community, and self-sufficiency that extends beyond the kitchen.

In a supportive and safe environment, our employed youth will build self-confidence, teamwork, and leadership skills. Ultimately, the program prepares youth for long-term success in their baking careers and personal lives.

Sketching Process

Initially, the visuals started as simple sketches of hands. However, after receiving feedback from peers, it became clear that creating a full character would be more impactful. This shift helped build a stronger emotional connection to the program and made the mural more engaging.

Developing Process

As for the logo and pattern work, it was a longer, more meticulous process. Choosing the right colours and positioning them thoughtfully took time to ensure the design felt balanced and didn’t overwhelm the packaging or other collateral materials.
