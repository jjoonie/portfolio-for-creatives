Just Socks Campaign

Fund Campaign Project

The Just Socks Foundation is a non-profit campaign that focuses on combating homelessness by distributing new socks to individuals in need. Acknowledging that socks are often overlooked in donation efforts, the foundation works to provide this essential item to enhance the comfort and dignity of those experiencing homelessness.

Through community sock drives and advocacy, it aims to raise awareness about the challenges faced by this vulnerable population.
