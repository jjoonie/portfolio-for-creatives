    Hello, lovely fellas :)

    I’m Jessica Baik! I’m an Korean-Vancouver based illustrator and designer, and I’m all about crafting visuals that tell meaningful stories. My art is shaped by the fictional worlds I adore, my cultural background, and the moments and experiences that stick with me. I love making art that sparks emotion and leaves people feeling inspired.

    I’m always excited to try something new! Pushing myself creatively is where I thrive, and I love exploring ideas that challenge me in the best way.

    If you are also an adventurous creative that wants to make imaginative projects with me, Let’s talk!
