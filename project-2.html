Osaka Food Trail

A visually captivating map that takes travelers on a culinary journey through Osaka, focusing on Dotonbori. Highlight the city’s vibrant food scene, featuring local dishes and unique flavors. The map will combine eye-catching illustrations with helpful details to inspire food lovers to explore Osaka’s must-try eats and make it a top destination for global travelers.
